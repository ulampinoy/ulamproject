Beef Mushroom Broccoli

Fri, Sep 2, 2011

Pinoy noodles sauteéd with vegetable, pork belly and soy sauce

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 kg beef
  • 1⁄4 kg oyster mushroom
  • 1⁄2 kg broccoli
  • 3 gloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tbs. oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1⁄2 cold water
  • salt and pepper
  • quail eggs for garnish
