Napakasarap at madaling lutuing beef stir-fry na saktong-sakto ulam.

### BEEF at MARINADE:
* 1 kg. beef
* 2 buong bawang (12-15 butil)
* 4 tbs. Chinese cooking wine
* 3/4 cornstarch
* 1 tb. baking soda
* 1 itlog
* pamintang durog
* 1 L cooking oil para sa pritong-lubog (deep-fry)

### SAUCE:
* 1/2 cup soy sauce
* 3 tbs. mirin
* 1/4 cup sugar

### GARNISH:
* Green onion
* Roasted sesame seeds
