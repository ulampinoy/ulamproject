Easy to make delicious and creamy spaghetti carbonara dish. Kids love it and great for your next party!

### INGREDIENTS:
* 500 grams Spaghetti
* 250 grams bacon, cut into strips
* 100 grams button mushroom
* 2-3 cloves garlic
* 1 onion
* 2 eggs
* cooking cream
* parsley
* salt & black pepper
